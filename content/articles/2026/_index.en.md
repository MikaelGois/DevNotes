## Welcome to 2026!

The year 2026 corresponds to the years 4722–4723 in the Chinese calendar, since the new year begins on February 17, 2026, being the year of the horse, and to the period between 5786 and 5787 in the Jewish calendar, given that Rosh Hashanah 2026 occurs in September, beginning the year 5787.


100 years ago (according to Wikipedia in Portuguese):
* Washington Luís is elected president of Brazil.
* Queen Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom, monarch and head of state of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, is born.
* Milton Santos, Brazilian geographer, writer, journalist, lawyer, professor, and scientist, is born.
* Marilyn Monroe, American actress, is born.
* Fidel Castro, first minister and later president of Cuba, is born.
* Roald Amundsen flies over the North Pole in the airship Norge.
* Erwin Schrödinger proposes the Schrödinger equation, which provides the mathematical basis for the wave model of atomic structure.
